Nehir and I meet from time to time to talk. We drink tea. Sometimes deep, sometimes silly. Always true.

Episode 01

Supermarket soap and what we do not know

Nehir isn’t complaining about supermarket soap—“What’s the harm?” she says. I’m asking the same question out of curiosity: what’s inside, who wrote that list? Two voices at one table; nobody is giving a lesson; the talk opens like that.
