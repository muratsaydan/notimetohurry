In brief

I studied here,
worked there,
then came home to Mersin.

Writing about myself feels strange; I am not someone who likes being on display. I wanted to fill this space plainly, without rushing it.

I spent time in Istanbul and a long while in London — reading, working, then returning to Mersin. I am only beginning to lean into slow living; I try to live that way and I am still learning what fits.

I love to read; philosophy has pulled at me for years. I keep returning to Rumi — the Masnavi travels with me. Eastern thought matters too: Tao, Zen, the quieter schools — they are part of how I slow down. I will not perform expertise; they are simply what holds me still.

What I want here is not a manifesto: a few notes on slow living while I am still a beginner, and perhaps a little quiet company for anyone who carries the same tiredness.
