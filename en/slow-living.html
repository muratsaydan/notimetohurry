On slow living

Speed is not a virtue,
it is a habit.

Modern life hurries us. We hurry too — because everyone does. Pausing now feels like weakness. I do not believe that.

一

Slowing down

Slowness is not laziness. There are things you only see when you slow down — things that used to pass you while you ran.

二

Knowing what you apply

Do you know what is inside what you put on your skin? Many of us do not. That not-knowing costs us.

三

Simplicity

A simple life is not poverty. It is knowing what truly matters. That distinction changes everything.
